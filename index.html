BeingStacked – Where Fresh Meets Fast (And Smart)

BeingStacked.com is a concept-driven online grocery platform created as part of an academic project for ITM Skills University – Semester 2, 2025. Designed to explore real-world user experience and grocery tech workflows, BeingStacked simulates a smart, rapid-delivery supermarket tailored for India’s evolving digital landscape.

It is inspired from BigBasket, BeingStacked proudly integrates with GRM Overseas Ltd., a globally reputed basmati rice exporter, and its consumer-facing brand 10X—known for premium rice, atta, pulses, and ready-to-cook offerings. This collaboration reflects our focus on quality-first products and real-scenario market insights.

Though fictional, the platform mimics real-time inventory, discount logic, and delivery modeling: browse curated fresh vegetables, fruits, dairy, and snacks, categorized for clarity.

Experience a 27-minute delivery promise—themed to test UI/UX concepts for quick commerce. Access convenient delivery slots from early mornings to late evenings, and interact with intelligent modules like “Har Din Sasta” and dynamic combo deals.

BeingStacked does not currently operate commercially but functions as a prototype showcasing design thinking, frontend integration, and product categorization within the fast-paced grocery sector. The goal? Learning by building something that feels real.
